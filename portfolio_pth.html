Portfolio - playingthehorn

playingthehorn is a personal creative project i began producing in 2025. initially, playingthehorn videos were an opportunity to construct a portfolio of videos exemplifying and improving my video and audio editing skills (premiere pro and audacity). now, the project has become an outlet to practice working in front of a camera. I have personal aspirations of being an articulate, precise and comfortable journalist down the line, and playingthehorn has become my batting cage to do so without expectations or boundaries. whilst these videos act as a creative outlet for me to release my thoughts and perspective on my passions, they also still offer insight into my skills as an editor overall. below are all videos released thus far to the channel, subject to additions, starting at my most recent.
