Stu's Studio (so far)

Stu On The Street is an interactive content series designed, written and edited by myself. Considering CFRE is the resident University of Toronto campus radio station, our team aimed to produce content interacting with the student body. My strategy was to introduce a series where students could see their friends and themselves featured, in turn promoting shares, likes and reposts. The content has gradually evolved from its first iteration, increasing production value and thematic representation of the studio's mascot, Stu. Attached are the four episodes thus far, progressing from the most recent to the very first episode.
